About

Who
Am I?

Mohammed Sahal is a business consultant, entrepreneur, and strategic thinker focused on helping businesses grow with clarity and structure. He is the founder of Team AE LLP and works closely with startups, entrepreneurs, and growing companies.

With a strong interest in business development, branding, and systems thinking, Sahal believes that great businesses are built not just on ideas, but on disciplined execution and strong financial foundations.

What
I Do

As a consultant, Sahal helps businesses build strong foundations in finance, compliance, and strategy. He supports organizations in transforming ideas into sustainable and scalable enterprises.

Beyond consulting, he is actively involved in building and scaling multiple ventures across various industries — including veterinary services, hospitality, and technology platforms — combining practical business insight with a long-term vision.
